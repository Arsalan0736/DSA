# Merge Two Sorted Linked Lists

## Problem Statement
Given the heads of two **sorted linked lists**, merge them into a single sorted linked list and return the head of the merged list.

---

## Examples

**Example :**
Input:
head1 = 5 -> 10 -> 15 -> 40
head2 = 2 -> 3 -> 20

Output:
2 -> 3 -> 5 -> 10 -> 15 -> 20 -> 40
